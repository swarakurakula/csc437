What Happens When Fictional Characters Meet Their Creators?

Categories: Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Prompt:A mysterious portal brings literary characters face-to-face with the authors who created them. Sherlock Holmes questions Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s ending choices, a superhero confronts their creator about their tragic backstory, and villains protest their portrayal. Can these creators handle the criticism, or will the characters write a new chapter of their own?
